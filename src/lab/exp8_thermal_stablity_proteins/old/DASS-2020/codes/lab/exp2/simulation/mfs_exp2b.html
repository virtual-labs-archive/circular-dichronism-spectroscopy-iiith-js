Manual

  1. Click on SOLUTION to bring it to the experiment table.
  2. Click on the CUVETTE to bring it to the experiment table
  3. Click on the PIPPETTE to bring it to the table
  4. Click on PIPPETTE to take solution from flask
  5. Click on PIPPETTE to make it out of the flask and come over the cuvette.
  6. Click on the pipette again to transfer the solution into the cuvette.
  7. Click on power button(green) to start the spectrofluorimeter. 30 min for the Initialization for the instrument
  8. Click on the lid of sample chamber of the Spectrometer to open it for placing the sample in the instrument.
  9. Click on the cuvette to place it in the sample holder of the chamber.
  10. Close the lid of the sample chamber by clicking on the lid.
  11. Click on the Computer Screen
